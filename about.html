About Langui

Langui was inspired by the desire to bridge communication gaps across different cultures and languages. As a developer, my passion for language learning and technology motivated me to create a real-time translation tool that could help people connect, no matter their native language.

This project was developed as part of my portfolio project at Holberton School, where I honed my skills in web development and programming.
